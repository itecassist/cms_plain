## Common Editing Tasks

### Changing Your Phone Number
1. Edit your homepage
2. Find the section labeled "Header Phone"
3. Change the number
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Updating Business Hours
1. Edit the page with your hours
2. Find "Header Hours" or "Business Hours"
3. Change the times
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Editing Main Page Text
1. Select the page you want to edit
2. Find the section (like "Hero Title" or "Welcome Text")
3. Type your new text
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Making Text Bold or Italic
1. Highlight the text you want to change
2. Click the **B** button for bold or **I** button for italic
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Adding a Link
1. Highlight the text that should be clickable
2. Click the üîó link button in the toolbar
3. Type or paste the web address (URL)

## Working with Images

### Uploading New Images
1. Click **"Manage Images"** from the dashboard
2. Click the upload area OR drag and drop your image
3. Wait for "Upload successful" message
4. Your image is now ready to use!

### Using an Uploaded Image
1. Upload your image first (see above)
2. Click the **"Copy Path"** button under your image
3. Go back to edit your page
4. Click where you want the image
5. Click the üñºÔ∏è image button in the toolbar
6. Paste the path you copied
7. Click OK
8. Click "Save Changes"

### Image Tips
- **Best image size:** 1920 x 1080 pixels for large images
- **Keep file size under 5MB**
- **Use .jpg for photos, .png for logos**
- **Name your files clearly** (e.g., "gym-equipment.jpg")

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't see my changes!"
- Make sure you clicked **"Save Changes"**
- Try refreshing your browser (press F5)
- Clear your browser cache

### "The text looks weird"
- You may have deleted a closing tag (</tag>)
- Click "Cancel" and try again
- Or contact your web developer

### "I can't upload images"
- Check your image is under 5MB
- Try a different image format (.jpg or .png)
- Make sure it's actually an image file

### "I forgot my password"
- Contact your web developer to reset it
- They can create a new password for you

### "The page won't save"
- Make sure you're still logged in
- Try logging out and back in
- Check your internet connection

## Best Practices

### Before You Make Big Changes:
1. **Take a screenshot** of the current version
2. Make your changes
3. Preview to make sure it looks good
4. If something goes wrong, you have the screenshot for reference

### Regular Maintenance:
- **Update content monthly** to keep it fresh
- **Check all pages quarterly** for outdated info
- **Delete old uploaded images** you're no longer using
- **Update contact info immediately** if it changes

### Writing for the Web:
- **Keep paragraphs short** (3-4 lines)
- **Use headings** to break up text
- **Write clearly** - avoid jargon
- **Include keywords** your customers search for
- **Add calls to action** - "Call Now", "Book Today", etc.
